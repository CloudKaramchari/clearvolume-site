Why this exists

The market-leading keyword-data extension has a large installed base and a strong average rating — the underlying feature is clearly wanted. But recent reviews cluster around business-practice complaints, not the data itself: an undisclosed ~8x price increase on existing customers, purchased credits that silently expire after 12 months with no warning at checkout, and confusing billing when users try to dispute either. ClearVolume isn't a better keyword algorithm — it's the same utility delivered without the ambush.

What it does

A small card, right under your search results

ClearVolume adds search volume, cost-per-click, and a 0–100 difficulty score directly under your Google search box for whatever you just searched — no separate dashboard, no tab-switching. Expand the card for up to 10 related keywords with the same metrics, sorted by volume.

  • Inline volume, CPC & difficulty on every Google search
  • Expandable related-keywords table, sorted by volume
  • 50 free lookups/day with a visible running counter
  • Paid credits roll over instead of expiring
  • Pricing locked in at signup — no surprise hikes
  • No tracking, no ads, nothing leaves your device beyond an active lookup
